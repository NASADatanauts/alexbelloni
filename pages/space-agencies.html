NASA Space Apps Challenge has the support of 10 space agencies, now. These agencies are from USA, Canada, Japan, Europe, Australia, Brazil, Argentina, Paraguay, Bahrain, and South Africa. The complete list and a searching tool of agencies are here.
Space agencies are government agencies engaged in activities related to outer space and space exploration. They are responsible for technical support for government, space programs, and scientific research.
As benefits of space exploration, we can mention experiments performed in space which help us understand health problems on the Earth. Furthermore, benefits to improve our day-to-day lives such as technologies for weather forecasts and communications, and encouragement for young people to study science, technology, engineering, and mathematics that is one of the goals of this hackathon.
Agencies develop some important activities, as well. Recently, we see Mars exploration with Perseverance rover, International Space Station research related to DNA repair, and climate observation using Earth satellites that is crucial to understand the urgent climate crisis.
